Help Page

General Functionalities

  • Add events of 4 different types to each day!
  • Switch through months with the next/prev buttons or use the selector!
  • Events are maintained even if you go to a different month!
  • The delete and edit buttons allow you to modify your events/days!

How to operate the delete button!

  1. After inputting some events, click on a day to highlight it.
  2. Click the delete button to toggle it on. Green means it's active.
  3. At the bottom is a scrollable list of events for that day you highlighted.
  4. Click on an event to delete it!
  5. Click the delete button again to turn it off.

How to operate the edit button!

  1. After inputting some events, click on a day to highlight it.
  2. Click the edit button to toggle it on. Green means it's active.
  3. At the bottom is a scrollable list of events for that day you highlighted.
  4. Click on an event to highlight it pink for editing!
  5. Now it's content will show in the input area. Change the category to your liking and edit the text.
  6. Press enter to change the event!
  7. Click the edit button again to turn it off.
